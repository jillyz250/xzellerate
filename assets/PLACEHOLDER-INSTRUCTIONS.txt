XZELLERATE - Image Asset Instructions
=====================================

Please add the following images to this folder:

1. logo.png
   - Your company logo
   - Recommended size: 200x60px
   - Format: PNG with transparent background
   - High resolution (2x for retina displays)

2. headshot.jpg
   - Professional headshot photo
   - Recommended size: 800x800px minimum
   - Format: JPG or WebP
   - High quality, professional lighting
   - Used in hero section

3. about-image.jpg
   - Professional photo for about section
   - Recommended size: 600x800px or larger
   - Format: JPG or WebP
   - Can be same as headshot or different pose
   - Used in about/credentials section

IMAGE OPTIMIZATION TIPS:
- Compress images before upload (use TinyPNG.com or Squoosh.app)
- Target file size: < 500KB per image
- Use WebP format for better compression
- Ensure images are sharp and professional quality
- Consider 2x resolution for retina displays

WHERE TO EXTRACT FROM WEBSITE:
If you have access to xzellerate.com:
1. Right-click on logo and "Save image as..."
2. Right-click on headshot and "Save image as..."
3. Save with the exact names listed above

TEMPORARY PLACEHOLDER:
Until you add your images, the site will show broken image icons
or use the text logo "XZELLERATE" as fallback.

The website is fully functional without images, but adding them
will significantly improve the professional appearance.
